About me:

Hello! I'm Natalia (or natu), a brazilian illustrator and concept artist, while also learning about being an animator and front end developer! I'm 27 years old, I'm from Sao Paulo but I'm living in Rio de Janeiro now. I love web design, creating fun characters and learn new things to improve my art and my skills. I've always worked as a freelance illustrator! I already made drawings for sites, games and clothes! This is my first time dealing with web design and creating my own websites.

I like to be able to share what I do with everyone! My goal is to go into character design, but I'm open to explore and try out new things and looking for opportunities I wouldn't even thought before.
